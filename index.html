Find the right home — anywhere

Every listing — rental or for sale — scored 0–100 on commute, cost, parking & internet, privacy, timing and trust, then plotted against up to three places you choose (work, school, family) with 5 km and 10 km rings. Set yours with 📍 My places, filter, sort, click a pin, open the listing or route.

How the board works

Vol 1 = core budget fits; Vol 2 = stretch/lifestyle options. Highlighted listings were added this round. The location score weights distance to all your places, with Place 1 counting most — so a home near everything beats one near only the gym.

Hover any score for its full component breakdown. Orange Confirm chips mark details the listing never stated — the score is honest about what's still unknown, and a missing must-have (location, price, parking for rentals) caps it. For-sale listings score on possession and property type instead of internet and roommates. Archive anything dead with the trash icon. The spreadsheet is the source of truth — edit it, then hit Refresh.

Where to search — sites & filters

Generic, widely-available listing sources for rentals and homes for sale. Apply the same filter discipline everywhere, and prefer direct listing pages over search pages.
ⓘ
Honest notes: map pins are exact where an address was given, otherwise a neighbourhood centroid (so a few sit at the area's middle). Distances are straight-line and drive times are estimates — confirm in Google Maps at your real commute time. Orange chips mark details a listing never stated; each says what to check before you commit. Some classifieds entries point to a live search page because their detail pages block automated reads. The spreadsheet is the source of truth — edit it, then hit Refresh data.
📍 My places

Up to three anchors — work, school, family. Listings get distances, map rings, route links and location scores measured against them (Place 1 counts most). Coordinates: hit Set on map then click the map, or right-click a spot in Google Maps and copy them.
